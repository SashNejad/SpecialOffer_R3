FAQ

Common questions

Do I need access to a gym, or can I work out at home?

Your program is built around whatever equipment you have — whether that's a full gym, a set of dumbbells at home, or just your bodyweight. Let me know during your assessment call and the plan will reflect it exactly.

How long before I start seeing results?

Everyone starts from a different place, so timelines vary. Most clients notice more energy, better sleep, and growing confidence in the first weeks; meaningful physical change builds with consistent effort over the following months. The key is showing up — the plan handles the rest.

Is this coaching only available in person?

No — coaching is fully online and available worldwide. The onboarding session can be done virtually over video call. If you're in Montreal, an in-person option is also available upon request.

What happens after I fill out the assessment?

Right after you submit, you'll pick a time for your free 15-minute call. I review your answers personally before we talk, so the conversation is about you — your goals, your schedule, your starting point. No commitment required, just an honest conversation.
